# 🌍 GEOGRAPHIC EXPANSION ROADMAP

## Overview
To scale TSTR.directory internationally, we are moving from a single-body approach (A2LA) to a **Multi-Body (AB) Aggregator Strategy**. This involves targeting regional cooperation bodies and national directories.

---

## 🎯 Primary Strategic Path: IAF CertSearch
The **IAF CertSearch API** is the official global database for management system certifications (ISO 9001, 14001, 45001, etc.) and aggregates data from major ILAC signatories including UKAS, DAkkS, and A2LA.

*   **Priority**: High (Scalability)
*   **Action**: Register for API access at `iafcertsearch.org/api`.
*   **Benefit**: Instant access to thousands of verified international certifications.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 1: Europe (EA)
Europe has the highest density of specialized testers.

1.  **ScopeMatch.eu (Aggregator)**: 
    *   Targets 10,017 labs across 24 countries.
    *   Aligns perfectly with TSTR's "Test Method" focus.
2.  **National Bodies**:
    *   **UKAS (UK)**: No open API, requires contact for high-volume access or custom scraper.
    *   **DAkkS (Germany)**: Manual search database; critical for Materials & Automotive.

---

## 🛠️ Phase 2: Middle East (ARAC)
High growth in Hydrogen and Oil & Gas infrastructure.

1.  **ENAS (UAE)**:
    *   Source: `moiat.gov.ae`.
    *   Status: **COMPLETE**. PoC verified.
2.  **GAC (GCC Accreditation Center)**:
    *   Source: `gcc-accreditation.org`.
    *   Status: **COMPLETE**. Scaled to Saudi, Qatar, UAE.

---

## 🏗️ Specialized Hub: Saudi Energy & Materials
The Middle East expansion (Phase 2) will prioritize the **Saudi Energy Testing Hub** due to the high density of Aramco/SEC approved facilities.

### 1. Strategic Data Sources
*   **Saudi Electricity Company (SEC)**: List of Qualified Laboratories (Jan 2026).
*   **Saudi Aramco**: Regulated Vendor List (RVL) for TPI and Laboratory services.
*   **SASO**: National petroleum product standards.

### 2. Priority Anchors (Seeded)
*   **GCC Lab (Dammam)**: High-voltage and electrical infrastructure.
*   **Al-Hoty Stanger**: General materials and chemical testing.
*   **ETLCO**: Environmental compliance and gas analysis.

### 3. Implementation
*   Develop a **Specialized Energy PSEO landing page** targeting "Aramco Approved Labs in Saudi Arabia" and "SEC Qualified Testing Facilities."

---

## 🛠️ Phase 3: Asia-Pacific (APAC)
Critical for manufacturing and supply chain verification.

1.  **NATA (Australia)**: World-class directory for materials and environmental testing. **Blocked by bot protection**; moved to IAF API path.
2.  **CNAS (China)**: The largest repository of accredited labs globally; requires high-fidelity parsing for translations.
