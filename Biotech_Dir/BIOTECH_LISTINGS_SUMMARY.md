## Key Biotech/Pharma Certifications & Quality Standards

### Regulatory Compliance
- **FDA 21 CFR Part 211** - Current Good Manufacturing Practice for Finished Pharmaceuticals
- **FDA 21 CFR Part 210** - cGMP requirements for biologics and biotech products
- **FDA 21 CFR Part 820** - Quality System Regulation for medical devices
- **ICH Q7** - Good Manufacturing Practice Guide for Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients
- **MHRA License** - UK Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency approval

### Quality Management Systems
- **ISO 13485** - Medical Devices - Quality Management Systems (CRITICAL for medical devices)
- **ISO 17025** - General Requirements for Competence of Testing and Calibration Laboratories
- **ISO 9001** - Quality Management Systems (foundational standard)
- **GMP** - Good Manufacturing Practice (pharmaceutical manufacturing)
- **GLP** - Good Laboratory Practice (preclinical safety testing)

### Testing Standards
- **USP <797>** - Pharmaceutical Compounding - Sterile Preparations
- **USP <71>** - Sterility Tests (microbiological testing)
- **USP <1046>** - Cellular and Tissue-Based Products (cell/gene therapy)
- **ISO 10993** - Biological Evaluation of Medical Devices (biocompatibility)
- **ISO 20387** - Biotechnology - Biobanking - General Requirements
- **ISO 13408** - Aseptic Processing of Health Care Products

### Market-Specific
- **CE Mark** - European Conformity marking for medical devices
- **CE-IVD** - CE marking for In Vitro Diagnostic devices
- **CLIA** - Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments (US lab certification)
- **UKAS** - UK Accreditation Service
- **CPA** - Clinical Pathology Accreditation (UK)
- **FDA 510(k)** - Premarket notification for medical devices

## Biotech-Specific Strategy: Open Access

### Why Open Access for Biotech?

**Trust Barrier**: Biotech/pharma industry is highly regulated and risk-averse
- Engineers won't pay for unverified listings
- Need to see FULL data before contacting
- Certifications are make-or-break (no ISO 13485 = no consideration)

**Discovery Problem**: Unlike hydrogen (searches by ISO standard), biotech searches by:
- Test type: "HPLC testing," "cell viability assay," "biocompatibility ISO 10993"
- Molecule type: "protein characterization," "antibody testing"
- Phase: "preclinical testing," "GMP release testing"

**Competitive Landscape**:
- **Contract Laboratory**: Free directory, massive listings, no paywall
- **PharmSource**: Paid but comprehensive, trusted by Big Pharma
- **Scientific.net**: Open access directory

**Strategy**: 
1. **Make ALL pharma/biotech listings 100% open** (no registration, no paywall)
2. **Differentiate on DATA QUALITY**:
   - Verified certifications (ISO 13485, GMP, FDA registrations)
   - Linked to specific test methods (HPLC, LC-MS, cell assays)
   - Turnaround times and pricing transparency
3. **Monetize via PREMIUM features**:
   - Labs pay for "Verified" badge
   - Labs pay for featured placement
   - Labs pay for RFQ lead forwarding
