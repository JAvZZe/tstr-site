## Executive Summary

Implemented hydrogen infrastructure testing as TSTR.site's primary niche, focusing on the high-margin, low-supply H2 certification market. The site is now the only directory indexed by specific hydrogen testing standards.

### Why Hydrogen Wins (First Principles)

1. **High Margin / Low Supply**: Standard labs cannot test at 700 bar (10,000 psi) or cryogenic hydrogen (-253°C). Capital equipment moat is massive.

2. **Chaos Multiplier**: Industry transitioning from natural gas to hydrogen. Manufacturers desperate because standard products fail (embrittlement) and cannot sell without complex certifications.

3. **Discovery Moat**: Engineers search by obscure ISO/SAE standard numbers (ISO 19880-3), not company brand. We're the only directory solving this.

## Competitive Moat

### Equipment Barriers
H2 testing requires capital-intensive infrastructure that standard labs don't have:

1. **Blast Bunkers**: For catastrophic failure testing (valves, tanks)
2. **Cryogenic Chambers**: Liquid hydrogen testing (-253°C)
3. **High-Pressure Autoclaves**: Up to 1000+ bar capability
4. **Embrittlement Test Rigs**: Materials qualification under H2 exposure

**Capital Cost**: $5M - $50M per facility

### Discovery Moat
- Engineers search "ISO 19880-3 testing" not "valve testing"
- Competitors (ThomasNet, Kompass) use generic categories
- TSTR.site is indexed by exact standard numbers
- SEO advantage on long-tail technical keywords

### Knowledge Moat
- Technical specifications visible (700 bar, -253°C, embrittlement)
- Detailed standard descriptions with failure mode context
- Engineers can self-qualify labs without sales calls
